Local Markets Partnership

Our partnership with local markets allows us to collect surplus produce and packaged goods that would otherwise be discarded. This collaboration has helped us rescue over 10,000 lbs of food monthly.

  • Daily surplus collection
  • Quality assurance protocols
  • Community impact reporting
Volunteer Couriers Network

Our dedicated volunteer couriers ensure timely and safe delivery of collected food to distribution centers. With over 200 active volunteers, we maintain a reliable transportation network.

  • Certified safe driving training
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Recognition programs
Community Kitchens Collaboration

Partnering with community kitchens, we provide fresh ingredients and support meal preparation for vulnerable populations. This partnership serves over 1,000 meals daily.

  • Nutritional meal planning
  • Kitchen equipment support
  • Staff training programs
Farm Partners Alliance

Local farms contribute excess harvest and unsold produce, helping us maintain a steady supply of fresh, nutritious food for our distribution network.

  • Seasonal harvest support
  • Sustainable farming practices
  • Farm-to-community direct links
Restaurant Alliance

Restaurant partners donate prepared meals and ingredients at the end of service, reducing waste while providing ready-to-eat options for our beneficiaries.

  • End-of-day surplus collection
  • Food safety compliance
  • Menu planning collaboration
Educational Partners

Schools and universities participate in our education programs, teaching students about food waste reduction and community service through hands-on learning experiences.

  • Curriculum integration
  • Student volunteer programs
  • Research partnerships
